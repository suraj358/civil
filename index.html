DRY DENSITY OF SOIL BY SAND REPLACEMENT METHOD

Objective

To determine the dry density of soil by sand replacement method.


Apparatus used:

Sand pouring cylinder, Cylindrical calibrating container, Vernier caliper, Weighing scale.

STEP  1 

Height and diameter of the sand pouring cylinder(SPC).

Height of cylinder(h) = _____

Diameter of cylinder(d) = _____

Volume of cylinder = _____

Volume of Cylinder = π/4 * (d2h)

Internal height of the cylinder = 12.5cm



Internal diameter of the cylinder = 10cm

Volume of cylinder =

✔ ✗ Formula Volume of cylinder =π/4*(d2h)

981cm3

STEP  2 

Fill the sand pouring cylinder (SPC) with sand and find its weight (W1).

STEP  3 

Place the SPC on a glass plate, open the slit above the cone and the sand torun down and the weight requiredto fill the cone(W2)g

The sand will freely run down till it fills the conical portion. When there is no further downward movement of sand in the SPC, close the slit. Measure the weight of the sand required to fill the cone. Let it be W2.

STEP  4 

Clean and level the soil to be tested.

STEP  5 

Open the slit of the SPC and allow the sand to run into the pit freely, till there is no downward movement of sand level in the SPC and then close the slit.

After 24 hours
